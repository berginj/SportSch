---
title: "Code of Conduct"
summary: "Expectations for players, coaches, and families at all AGSA activities."
order: 1
lastUpdated: "2026-02-07"
---

AGSA expects respect, effort, and sportsmanship from everyone on and off the field.

- Treat players, coaches, officials, and families with respect.
- No abusive language, harassment, or threatening behavior.
- Support positive sideline behavior and player development.
- Follow coach and league direction on game day logistics and safety.

If a concern occurs, report it to a board member using the contact model on `/contact`.
